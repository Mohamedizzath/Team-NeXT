Attractions Nearby

The travel destination Uda Walawe is one of the biggest tourist attractions in the wildlife adventure in Sri Lanka. Few years back it was only a day-trip to the Uda Walawe National Park or just a stopover on a way to East coast or to Kataragama. Breathtaking Udawalawe reservoir with the backdrop of the Southern slope of the Rajawake, Uggalkaltota Mountains and the countless acres of the National Park add more glamour to the newly built resorts in the area.
